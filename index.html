Today Pic Windows 10 App



Today Pic is a windows 10 universal app that shows you the Bing Picture of the day. You can download it, set as wallpaper or set as lock screen wallpaper. You can also set the nation and the orientation (landscape or portrait).

Did you miss one picture of the day? Visit the gallery page, where you can find the pictures of the last two years. If you run this app on your phone, you can even preview how the new picture will look like with your opacity settings. The developer isn't the owner of the pictures. All the rights are reserved by the Photographer and Microsoft® Bing. This app is not affiliated with Bing or its services in any way.
